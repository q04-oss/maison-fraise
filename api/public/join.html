how it works
01
business creates an event
title, description, seat range. no date. the experience is the draw, not the calendar slot.
02
invitations go out
business selects members from their interest list and the wider pool. selected members receive a push notification.
03
members respond
accept spends one akène. decline costs nothing. the business watches responses in real time.
04
threshold met
once enough people accept, the business sets the date. confirmed members are notified. the room is set.
