Orders
How do I place an order?
Download the box fraise app, select a strawberry variety from the Board, choose your chocolate and finish, pick a collection window at Marché Atwater, and pay. You'll receive a confirmation email and a push notification when your order is ready.
Where do I collect my order?
At the chocolate counter inside Marché Atwater — 138 Av. Atwater, Montréal. We are there every morning, 9:00–17:00.
How will I know when my order is ready?
You'll receive a push notification and an email when your order is ready for collection. Make sure notifications are enabled: iOS Settings → box fraise → Notifications → Allow Notifications.
Can I cancel or modify my order?
Email us at austin@fraise.maison as soon as possible. We can accommodate changes before the order begins preparation. Once dipping has started, changes are not possible.
Can I get a refund?
If there's a problem with your order — wrong items, quality issue, or if you were unable to collect — email us and we will make it right. Refunds are handled case by case.
My order history isn't showing up.
Go to the Orders tab and enter the exact email address you used when placing the order. If you signed in with Apple, your order may be linked to a private relay address — email us and we'll locate it.
Membership
What is the membership?
box fraise offers seven annual membership tiers — from Découverte to Or Absolu — funded upfront each year. Membership unlocks access to the social platform, editorial layer, greenhouse investment panels, and deeper features within the app.
How do I upgrade or manage my membership?
Open the app and go to your member profile. Tier upgrades are available directly from there. For billing questions or to cancel, email us at austin@fraise.maison.
What is the NFC time bank?
Each box you tap with your phone credits platform time to your account. Your social tier — Standard, Reserve, or Estate — is determined by how much time remains in your bank, not by a fixed subscription. Buying more frequently, or in larger lots, keeps your tier active longer.
My social access expired. How do I restore it?
Tap a new box. Access is restored immediately when your time bank has a positive balance. There is no grace period — the system is intentionally cut-throat. A standing order keeps you from lapsing.
Tokens & Currency
What is Fraise (FRS)?
Fraise is a gold-backed digital currency issued by Rajzyngier Research. One Fraise equals one London Good Delivery 400 troy ounce gold bar held in allocated Swiss vault storage. FRS runs on the Optimism network.
What are strawberry tokens?
Each box variety has a numbered series of generative SVG tokens. When you purchase a box, a token is minted and linked to that specific harvest. Tokens are tradeable within the platform and carry the full provenance of the variety they came from.
I have a question about a token transfer or balance.
Email us at austin@fraise.maison with your member code and the token number in question.
Account & App
Is my payment information secure?
Yes. Payments are processed by Stripe. We never see or store your card details. Stripe is certified to PCI Service Provider Level 1 — the highest level of payment security certification.
I'm not receiving notifications.
Check iOS Settings → box fraise → Notifications → Allow Notifications. You will also receive a ready email as a backup.
How do I delete my account?
Email us at austin@fraise.maison with your request. We will remove your account and associated data within 30 days. Note: token provenance records are anonymised rather than deleted, as they form part of the permanent harvest ledger.
