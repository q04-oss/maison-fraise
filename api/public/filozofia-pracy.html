Filozofia Pracy

conversations on the philosophy of work

A monthly long-form conversation podcast on labour, recognition, building, and the architectures that surround them. The title is Stanisław Brzozowski's — from his 1910 collection Idee, the first sustained philosophy of work in the Polish tradition. The conversations draw on that lineage and on the bodies of thought adjacent to it: feminist labour economics, post-Holocaust memory architecture, the ethics of solidarity.

The guests are people who have spent serious time on these questions. The format is slow and direct. The cadence is monthly.

interested in being a guest?

The show is looking for guests who have spent serious time thinking about work — its conditions, its recognition, its relationship to place and to memory. That includes philosophers, economists, architects, builders, and people whose lived experience speaks to the subject. Academic credentials are not required. Seriousness is.

If that is you or someone you know, write to austin@rajzyngier.co with a short note about who you are and what you've been working on.
