Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 30, 2026

Maison Fraise ("we", "us", "our") operates the Maison Fraise iOS application and the website at fraise.maison. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights with respect to it.

We collect only what is necessary to process your order. We do not sell your data. We do not use it for advertising.

Information we collect

When you place an order, we collect:

  • Email address — to send your order confirmation and pickup notification.
  • Order details — variety, chocolate, finish, quantity, collection time. Stored to fulfil your order and display your order history.
  • Payment information — handled entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your card number. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment data.
  • Push notification token — if you grant notification permission, we store a device token to send you a notification when your order is ready. You can revoke this at any time in iOS Settings.

How we use your information

  • To process and fulfil your order.
  • To send you a branded confirmation email and a ready notification when your order is prepared.
  • To display your order history in the app when you enter your email address.

We do not use your information for marketing, profiling, or any purpose beyond fulfilling your order.

Third-party services

  • Stripe — payment processing. stripe.com/privacy
  • Resend — transactional email delivery. resend.com/privacy
  • Expo — push notification infrastructure. expo.dev/privacy
  • Railway — server and database hosting. railway.app/legal/privacy

Data retention

Order records are retained for business and legal purposes. If you would like your data deleted, contact us at austin@fraise.maison and we will remove it within 30 days.

Children

The Maison Fraise app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at austin@fraise.maison.
